About

I'm a Raleigh-based published author and former English and programming teacher offering free, one-on-one help to students navigating college application. This includes: choosing schools, managing deadlines, and especially writing strong personal essays.

Meetings are remote, so we can start wherever you are.

What I help with

  • Building a college list

    Finding schools that fit your goals, strengths, and circumstances — reach, match, and safety.

  • Timelines & deadlines

    A clear, manageable plan so applications, tests, and forms get done without the last-minute scramble.

  • Essay & supplement coaching

    One-on-one help turning your story into a personal essay and supplements that actually sound like you.

  • First-gen & low-income guidance

    Strong communication and help navigating a process that wasn't built to be obvious, for families doing this for the first time.

Who this is for

This is free and open to any family who needs it, with particular priority and a warm welcome for first-generation and low-income students.
